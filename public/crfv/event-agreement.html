Event Participation Agreement

By registering for any Council for the Restoration of Filipino Values, Inc. (CRFV) event, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read this agreement carefully.

Last Updated: July 12, 2025

1. Media Release Authorization

By participating in this event, you grant Council for the Restoration of Filipino Values, Inc. (CRFV) the irrevocable, perpetual, and worldwide rights to:

  • Capture photographs, video recordings, and audio recordings during the event (both in-person and virtual sessions)
  • Use, reproduce, modify, and distribute such media in all promotional materials, publications, social media, websites, and any other platforms
  • Use your name, likeness, and voice in connection with such media

This authorization applies to all current and future media formats and platforms. No compensation or opt-out option is provided for media usage.

You understand that Council for the Restoration of Filipino Values, Inc. (CRFV) may use these materials for promotional, educational, and informational purposes without limitation.

2. Item Return Policy

Participants agree to return all provided items upon completion of the event. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Proximity cards or access devices
  • Training materials and equipment
  • Any other items provided by Council for the Restoration of Filipino Values, Inc. (CRFV) for the event

Failure to return items by the end of the event will result in a fine of ₱500.00 per item, payable within 15 days of the event's conclusion.

Items must be returned to the designated collection point at the event venue, or by prepaid mail if taken off-site.

3. Completion Certificate Requirements

Council for the Restoration of Filipino Values, Inc. (CRFV) reserves the right to withhold the issuance of completion certificates if participants fail to meet the following requirements:

  • Full Attendance: Participants must attend 100% of mandatory sessions (verified via attendance tracking)
  • Assessment Completion: Successful completion of all required assessments and assignments
  • Item Return: Return of all provided items as specified in Section 2
  • Code of Conduct: Adherence to all event rules and regulations

Certificates are expected to be issued within 7 business days after the event, provided all requirements are completed. While we aim to meet this timeline, unforeseen delays may occur beyond the organiser’s control.

4. Data Privacy Compliance

Council for the Restoration of Filipino Values, Inc. (CRFV) is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with:

  • The Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173)
  • International data protection standards where applicable
