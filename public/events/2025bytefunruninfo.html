Ready, Set, Innovate: Join the 2025 BYTe Fun Run!

"A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step," Lao Tzu reminds us. What better way to take that step than by joining the 2025 BYTe Fun Run? On February 28, 2025, this exciting event will bring together runners, innovators, and changemakers for an unforgettable morning at Benguet State University. This isn’t just about hitting the pavement—it’s about closing IT Month 2025 with purpose, energy, and community spirit.



Spearheaded by Body of Young Technologists (BYTe) students from the College of Information Sciences - Department of Information Technology, the event combines fitness and innovation, offering scenic 5KM and 3KM routes that showcase BSU’s vibrant campus. It’s more than just a run; it’s a celebration of health, technology, and the people who make them both thrive.


Register now to ensure your race bibs are ready on race day! Walk-in participants are welcome if you didn’t register, but please note that the availability of extra race bibs may be limited. Whether you're a seasoned runner aiming to beat your personal best or a first-timer looking for a reason to get moving, the BYTe Fun Run has a place for you. Mark your calendars, gather your team, and join us in this exciting event that blends health and innovation seamlessly.
