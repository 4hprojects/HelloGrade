2025 BYTe Fun Run: Step Up, Lace Up, and Celebrate Community


“Runners don’t just run—they inspire.” This sentiment rings especially true as we gear up for the 2025 BYTe Fun Run, a marquee event to cap off the IT Month Celebration at Benguet State University's College of Information Sciences, Department of Information Technology. This isn't just a run; it’s a celebration of community, health, and innovation.

Register Now

Happening on 28 February 2025, the BYTe Fun Run offers two exciting routes:

  • 5KM run: Begins at 5:50 AM, taking runners through the Men’s Dorm, PAGASA Access Road, and the College of Forestry, then passing through the College of Engineering and CHED checkpoints before looping back to the Last Gate and finishing at the CIS Building.

  • 3KM run: Starting at 6:00 AM, going through the College of Engineering and CHED checkpoints before looping back to the Last Gate and finishing at the CIS Building.

The race begins and ends at the CIS Building, with scenic routes through key university landmarks like the Clinic and Last Gate, creating an engaging and rewarding experience for runners.
