BYTe Fun Run 2025 Results: Race Highlights & Winners

Published on: March 4, 2025 by Henson M. Sagorsor

"It’s not about being the fastest. It’s about showing up, pushing forward, and finishing strong."

The BYTe Fun Run 2025 took place on February 28, 2025, attracting a total of 218 online sign-ups. On race day, 215 runners participated, with 58 registered participants unable to attend, and an additional 55 walk-in participants joining the event, ensuring a competitive and exciting race. Among the participants, 97 were members of the Body of Young Technologists (BYTe), while the remaining runners consisted of non-BYTe participants from various Benguet State University (BSU) colleges and the wider running community. The event showcased outstanding performances from both male and female runners, with special recognition given to those who overcame every challenge to reach the finish line.

Race Results: BYTe Fun Run 2025 Winners

The BYTe Fun Run 2025 showcased the endurance and determination of runners across two race categories: the 3K run and the 5K run. Runners were divided into BYTe participants (IT students) and non-BYTe participants from different BSU colleges and professional running communities.

🏅 Special Award: Most Determined Participant

The Most Determined Participant award was given to the runner who finished the race last but with unwavering determination and strength. This participant exemplified the true spirit of the BYTe Fun Run 2025, proving that every step forward is a victory.

Diverse Participation: More Than Just a Run

The BYTe Fun Run 2025 wasn’t just for IT students. The event welcomed runners from various disciplines, making it a truly inclusive and community-driven race. Enthusiastic participants from Benguet State University (BSU) joined alongside professional runners and fitness enthusiasts to celebrate health, endurance, and camaraderie.

🎓 BSU Colleges Represented

Students from different colleges at BSU laced up their running shoes and showed their competitive spirit. The following colleges had strong representation in the race:

  • College of Human Kinetics – Future fitness experts setting the pace.
  • College of Public Administration and Governance – Leaders who took on the challenge.
  • College of Home Economics and Technology – Showing that endurance is a life skill.
  • College of Engineering – Proving that perseverance applies both in and out of the classroom.
  • College of Arts and Humanities – Adding a creative touch to the event.
  • College of Teacher Education – Showcasing the strength and dedication of future educators.

🏃‍♂️ Professional Runners and Enthusiasts

Alongside student participants, experienced runners and fitness enthusiasts took on the challenge, making the event even more competitive and inspiring. These individuals pushed the pace, encouraging others to keep moving forward and cross the finish line with pride.

💡 A Run for Everyone

Whether participants were running to win, running for fun, or simply running to finish, the BYTe Fun Run 2025 proved to be an event where everyone belonged. The mix of students, professionals, and first-time runners created an environment of mutual encouragement, determination, and shared achievement.

Sponsors and Volunteers: The Backbone of BYTe Fun Run 2025

The BYTe Fun Run 2025 was more than just a race—it was a community effort made possible by the generous sponsors and the dedication of volunteers. Their contributions ensured that participants had the best experience, from seamless event execution to post-run refreshments.

🎉 Event Sponsors

A huge thank you to our sponsors, who provided essential event support, materials, and post-run treats. Their contributions played a vital role in making this event smooth, organized, and memorable.

  • hellograde.online – Powered event registration and provided online race updates.
  • Necro Thread – Produced high-quality race bibs and printed materials for all runners.
  • Picky Grinders – Kept runners energized with refreshing drinks after the race.
  • Jovelyn Lao-e – Sponsored freshly baked pandesal, giving participants a warm, post-run snack.

🙌 Dedicated Volunteers

A special shout-out to the IT student volunteers from BYTe, whose enthusiasm, hard work, and initiative made this event possible. From setting up the racecourse to assisting at water stations and handling registrations, their teamwork ensured that everything ran smoothly.

The success of the BYTe Fun Run 2025 is a testament to what can be achieved when students, sponsors, and the community come together for a common goal. Their efforts made the event more than just a run—it became a celebration of resilience, technology, and teamwork.
