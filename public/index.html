Welcome to HelloGrade

HelloGrade is an innovative, easy-to-use grading system by 4HProjects, tailored to enhance the student and teacher experience in educational institutions. It bridges the gap between manual grading processes and digital accuracy.

Efficient Grade Management

Teachers can input grades, track student progress, and make real-time adjustments, reducing administrative tasks.

Real-Time Student Access

Students can access their grades instantly, promoting responsibility and goal setting.

Customizable Grading Criteria

Supports point-based, percentage, and custom grading scales to match academic standards.

Data Security and Privacy

Ensures secure data handling with encryption and limited access to protect user information.

Why Choose HelloGrade?

HelloGrade is more than just a grading tool—it’s a comprehensive solution designed to support educators and empower students. With HelloGrade, institutions can simplify grade management, enhance transparency, and foster a proactive learning environment.

Developed with attention to detail and an understanding of educational needs, HelloGrade is here to support teachers, inspire students, and transform how grades are managed in today’s schools.
