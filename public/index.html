HelloGrade is an innovative, easy-to-use grading system designed by 4HProjects, tailored to enhance the student and teacher experience in educational institutions. Created as a personal project, HelloGrade bridges the gap between manual grading processes and digital accuracy, providing a seamless experience for students, teachers, and administrators.

What HelloGrade Offers

  • Efficient Grade Management: HelloGrade simplifies the process of recording, calculating, and distributing grades. Teachers can easily input grades, track student progress, and make real-time adjustments, reducing the time spent on administrative tasks.
  • Real-Time Student Access: Students can access their grades instantly, providing them with transparency and allowing them to track their academic performance across subjects. This instant access promotes responsibility and helps students set realistic goals based on their current standings.
  • Customizable Grading Criteria: Teachers can customize grading criteria to fit specific academic requirements or standards. Whether it's a point-based system, percentage-based grading, or custom scales, the system adapts to your institution’s grading policies.
  • Data Security and Privacy: Built with security as a priority, HelloGrade uses advanced data encryption and secure sessions to protect user information. Students’ academic data remains confidential and accessible only to authorized users, ensuring compliance with data privacy standards.
  • Seamless Integration: HelloGrade is built to integrate with common educational tools and systems. The platform supports easy data import/export functions and can work alongside other tools, simplifying the migration of student records and grade data.
  • Insights and Reports: Teachers and administrators can generate insightful reports that highlight class performance, individual progress, and other key metrics. These reports help in making data-driven decisions to support student success.

Why Choose HelloGrade?

HelloGrade is more than just a grading tool—it’s a comprehensive solution designed to support educators and empower students. With HelloGrade, institutions can simplify grade management, enhance transparency, and foster a proactive learning environment.

Developed with attention to detail and an understanding of educational needs, HelloGrade is here to support teachers, inspire students, and transform how grades are managed in today’s schools.
