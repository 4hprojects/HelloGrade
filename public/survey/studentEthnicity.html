Student Ethnicity & Cultural Identity Survey

This survey aims to understand the diverse ethnic backgrounds within our institution. The collected data will help promote inclusivity, support culturally responsive education, and enhance programs that acknowledge and respect different identities present in our student community.

It will take approximately 3-5 minutes to complete.

Data Privacy & Confidentiality:
  • Your responses will be strictly confidential and only used for institutional research and educational development purposes.
  • The collected data will be anonymized and analyzed in aggregate form.
  • Your information will be handled in accordance with data privacy laws and institutional policies.
By proceeding, you acknowledge understanding the purpose of this survey and agree to provide accurate information.
