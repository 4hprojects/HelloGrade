About Us

Welcome to HelloGrade, your all-in-one solution for efficient academic management and monitoring. Our platform is designed to revolutionize the way educational institutions, teachers, students, and parents interact and collaborate to ensure seamless communication and academic success.

Our Mission

At HelloGrade, we aim to bridge the gap between educators and learners by providing a user-friendly platform that streamlines administrative processes and fosters an engaging learning environment. Our mission is to simplify grade management, attendance tracking, and academic progress monitoring with innovative digital tools.

Our Vision

We envision a future where technology empowers education, making it more accessible, transparent, and efficient for everyone involved. HelloGrade strives to be a catalyst for educational excellence by integrating cutting-edge solutions into everyday academic operations.

Who We Are

HelloGrade was developed by a dedicated team of educators and IT professionals with years of experience in teaching, software development, and project management. Led by Henson M. Sagorsor, the CIS College Co-Sports Coordinator and a passionate advocate for educational innovation, our team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of academic needs.

What We Offer

  • Grade Monitoring: A centralized platform for managing and viewing academic performance.
  • Attendance Tracking: Tools for tracking student attendance with ease and precision.
  • Communication Hub: Features for seamless interaction between educators, students, and parents.
  • Customizable Tools: Flexible options to adapt to the specific needs of educational institutions.

Why Choose Us?

  • User-Centric Design: HelloGrade is built with simplicity and usability in mind.
  • Reliable and Secure: We prioritize data security and system reliability to safeguard sensitive academic information.
  • Dedicated Support: Our team is here to assist you every step of the way, ensuring a smooth and productive experience.
